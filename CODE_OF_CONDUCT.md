# 貢獻者行為準則 Contributor Code of Conduct

## 我們的承諾 Our Pledge

為了營造開放友善的環境，我們承諾讓參與本專案成為一個對所有人都無騷擾的體驗，不論年齡、體型、身心障礙、族裔、性別認同與表達、經驗水準、國籍、外貌、種族、宗教或性傾向。

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## 我們的準則 Our Standards

有助於正向環境的行為 Examples of positive behavior：

- 使用友善包容的語言。Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- 尊重不同觀點與經驗。Being respectful of differing viewpoints.
- 優雅地接受建設性批評。Gracefully accepting constructive criticism.
- 以社群最佳利益為重。Focusing on what is best for the community.

不可接受的行為 Unacceptable behavior：

- 帶有性意味的言語或圖像、騷擾。Sexualized language/imagery or harassment.
- 嘲諷、侮辱或人身攻擊。Trolling, insults, or personal attacks.
- 公開或私下騷擾。Public or private harassment.
- 未經許可公開他人私人資訊。Publishing others' private information without permission.

## 執行 Enforcement

如遇濫用、騷擾或其他不可接受行為，請聯絡專案維護者：
Instances of abusive behavior may be reported to the project maintainer:

維護者有權移除、編輯或拒絕不符本準則的留言、commit、程式碼、issue 與其他貢獻。
Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code of conduct.
